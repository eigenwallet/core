## How does eigenwallet work?

**eigenwallet** is a desktop application with support for the eigenwallet atomic swap protocol.
You can connect to other people also running the protocol.
_Market makers_ (or just "makers") will buy your Bitcoin and in exchange give you Monero.

Each swap is entirely peer-to-peer.
Every maker controls their own exchange rate.
You decide whom to swap with.
